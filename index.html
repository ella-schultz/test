Who We Represent

NFFE Local 1650 provides professional, contract-based representation to bargaining unit employees across Los Padres National Forest.

Our focus is forest-level advocacy. We ensure consistent enforcement of the Master Agreement, compliance with Forest Service directives, and accountability in workplace matters.

We are committed to structured representation, clear communication, and policy-driven advocacy.
